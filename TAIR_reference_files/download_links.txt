
The following files are necessary for many of the scripts in this repository to work;
however, given their large size, they have been excluded from the commits.
Luckily, you can download them yourself from the public TAIR ftp pages indicated below.

TAIR10 annotations:

  TAIR10_3_utr_20101028.fa
  TAIR10_5_utr_20101028.fa
  TAIR10_cdna_20101214_updated.fa
  TAIR10_cds_20101214_updated.fa
  TAIR10_seq_20101214_updated.fa
  can be obtained from:
  https://www.arabidopsis.org/tools/bulk/sequences/index.jsp
  https://www.arabidopsis.org/help/helppages/BLAST_help.jsp#datasets

  TAIR10_GFF3_genes.gff
